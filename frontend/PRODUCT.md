# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Campus buyers browsing, comparing, and purchasing daily goods through a Vue-based ecommerce app. They need quick search, clear product comparison, cart and checkout confidence, order tracking, and member services without visual friction.

## Product Purpose

Provide a demonstrable buyer-side ecommerce flow for a campus-selected mall: discovery, filtering, product detail, cart, checkout, simulated payment, delivery status, reviews, messages, and member utilities. Success means users can understand stock, price, promotions, status, and next actions at a glance.

## Brand Personality

Practical, fresh, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a capable campus shopping tool: light enough for daily use, direct in its actions, and calm around payment and order states.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS landing-page tropes, overly rounded ghost cards, purple default component styling, low-contrast gray text, side-stripe status markers, and heavy marketing visuals that compete with product information.

## Design Principles

Make shopping decisions scannable before they are beautiful.

Keep actions predictable: primary means progress, secondary means optional, danger means irreversible.

Use color for meaning: brand, status, price, and selection should not blur together.

Let dense commerce screens stay dense, but keep spacing and alignment consistent enough to reduce cognitive load.

Design mobile as a real shopping context, not a collapsed desktop afterthought.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve visible focus states, avoid motion-gated content, respect reduced-motion preferences, and keep touch targets comfortable on mobile.
